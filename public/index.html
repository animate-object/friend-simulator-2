I'm building a game about friendship. Meet unique (procedurally generated) humans, and befriend them through the power of listening comprehension.

The latest update connects the friend backend, which generates a 'world' of entities for each pageload, as well as connections between those entities. This world represents the body of knowledge unique to each friend. In the future, the friends will express more complex thoughts, and know more about their relation to the world.

Additionally, this changeset adds a color hashing algorithm which colors each unique noun phrase. It really makes them 'pop'.
